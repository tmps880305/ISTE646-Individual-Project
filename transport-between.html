High Speed Rail

Taiwan High Speed Rail is the fastest land transportation along the west coast of Taiwan. With 350 km/hr average speed, the Taiwan High Speed Rail could move from Taipei to Kaohsiung in around 2 hours, producing great convenience for people in Taiwan to travel between cities without taking airplanes or driving.

Comments
Rating

It’s a comfortable and time saving way to travel between cities. Though the price might be a little bit higher, the experience would definitely be worth it.

Taiwan Railway

Taiwan Railway is the main railway system that services the railway network throughout the entire Taiwan island. Similar to railways in many other countries, Taiwan Railway offers various types of trains for different speeds and destinations. The range of service is wider than High Speed Rail, and the price is more affordable.

Comments
Rating

This is a cheaper way to commute between cities, however, would be less comfortable. It’s suggested to travel in shorter distance or to places without High Speed Rail.

Driving

Driving is always a great way to travel in Taiwan. Highways and roads are well-developed across the island. However, due to the high density of population and travel needs during vacations, traffic jam happens frequently on highways or in cities, especially on specific holidays.
