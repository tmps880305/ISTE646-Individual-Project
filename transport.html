Between Cities

High Speed Rail

Taiwan High Speed Rail is the fastest land transportation along the west coast of Taiwan. With 350 km/hr average speed, the Taiwan High Speed Rail could move from Taipei to Kaohsiung in around 2 hours, producing great convenience for people in Taiwan to travel between cities without taking airplanes or driving.
