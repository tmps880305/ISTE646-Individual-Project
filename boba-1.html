The origin of Boba Tea

Chun Shui Tang

Chun Shui Tang is established in 1983, which is the first cold tea bar in Taiwan. The cold tea was then developed into various tea drinks that we could see today, and the boba tea was one of them.

The first Chun Shui Tang is introduced at Taichung with the old fashion style of store decoration, which allowed people to enjoy modern drinks in a traditional tea tasting environment. Until today, Chun Shui Tang keeps this tradition and serves customers with the same feeling of tea time in every store. Chun Shui Tang could be found in lots of cities in the west coast of Taiwan.
